About Me

Jose Pereda was born in a rural town in Jalisco, Mexico, in a region commonly known as tequila country. He as in parents immigrated to the United States in 1979. He and his six siblings were raised in the suburbs of Los Angeles. As of today, he is the only one of those siblings that decided to leave the nest and move to a different city. Married nearly 20 years. His wife and he have five children, in every age range. The eldest is 24 and the youngest just over two years of age. Up until a few years back Mr. Pereda managed business operations for a global conglomerate. Fist in the United States and later in Latin America. This fact provided the opportunity for him to visit a variety of country. And yes, tons of airports and hotels.
